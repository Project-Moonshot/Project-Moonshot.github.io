Dubai International Academy Model United Nations (DIAMUN) is a student-led conference based in Dubai committed to providing new and experienced student delegates with enriching MUN experiences. It is the largest private MUN conference in the MENA region, welcoming over 800 delegates from 50 schools all over the world to engage in challenging debates over economic, social, and political issues present in the world today. Our debates lay a concrete foundation for students to demonstrate their talents through voicing opinions and negotiating in an environment comparable to the United Nations. Moreover, engaging in discussions with people from diverse backgrounds encourages our delegates to take risks and be open-minded, creating a distinct and intellectual set of individuals.
