ECO Club

Clean the Air. Clean our Bodies. Clean the Earth.

The Eco Club is a student-led action, service and awareness group that aims to increase the environmental and ecological actions taken at DIA. Last year, we raised awareness of at present issues like mental health and paper wastage through a range of events and initiatives taken amongst the DIA community to encourage students to be more eco-friendly. This year, our aim is to focus on physical and mental wellbeing, water and energy consumption through internal and external activities and competitions. With the commitment to “Clean the Air. Clean our Bodies. Clean the Earth’, we have a group of over 40 enthusiastic ASA members and 12 board members who collaborate on a weekly basis.

We are passionate to provide a wonderful opportunity to create awareness, build attitudes and help students take up sustainable activities in the real world, beyond the classroom and curriculum. Eco Club at DIA organizes diverse, interactive activities to involve the DIA community for being environmentally conscious.

We have hosted big and successful events within the school to raise awareness of certain environmental issues and make the DIA community a more eco-friendly one. For instance, last year we held the Adopt-A-Plant day on the 22nd of April, which is Earth Day, where we provided students and teachers across primary and secondary part of the school, with plants to purchase and a chance to take a small step to create a bigger impact. Following this, we also initiated our first Wellness Wednesday, which was aimed to allow students to reflect on the choices they make in their lives. It was a day dedicated to allow each and every one of us to understand the potential benefits of physical and mental well-being through activities the Eco-Club has planned.
