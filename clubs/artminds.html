Art Minds

Art is Freedom

YouTube / artmindsglobal@gmail.com / Instagram / Pinterest

Art Minds is more than just a club, but is also a global organization. Our mission is to elevate the art scene and increase the presence of the global art community through hosting international competitions, which accept and embrace all forms of art, whether it is painting, photography, or digital design. Art Minds is a leading youth art organization which has already hosted an international sponsored competition which received hundreds of participants from countries in all 5 continents. Additionally, Art Minds caters to students tutorials and other art content on their socials, and will also be hosting after-school ASA's starting September 2020 at DIA, where students can go for support with their competition pieces, to learn techniques to implement, or just for inspiration and those whom have an interest.
