DIAlect

DIAlect aim's to inform the student body on a variety of topics: Student life, the local community, international news, and more!

ibrahim.shanqiti@gmail.com / Instagram

DIAlect was first created as a club that would inform the general public about both school events, and news around the world. From then on, it has now grown to be a creative outlet for students to submit their literature works for a chance to be featured on one of our newsletters, and this year we have taken on a new challenge: The DIAlect Podcast. This podcast aims to inform students about topics like the Head Team, and even entertaining interviews with teachers.
