Make Health Happen

Illness Is Universal, Yet Access To Healthcare Is Not.

makehealthappen@gmail.com / Instagram

MakeHealthHappen is an awareness and action club that aims to spread knowledge about the standard of health-care in LEDC's. We have a weekly news show with several segments to keep students updated about the worldwide happenings and much more. We strive to improve the knowledge of students in DIA and around the world.
